Types of research designs (3-6 hours)

↵ Back to class homepage

Module overview

The goal of this module is for you to understand what different types of study design are, and what their advantages and limitations are. By the end of the module, hopefully you will be able to do the following things:

  • Explain the difference between experimental and observational research, as well as their advantages and drawbacks;
  • Explain the difference between confirmatory and exploratory research, as well as their advantages and drawbacks;
  • (advanced) Classify results on the experimental/observational and confirmatory/exploratory dimensions.

Module instructions

This module includes two tasks (plus one task for advanced completion).

Module activities

  1. Experimental vs. observational research (3 hours)
  2. Exploratory vs. confirmatory research (2 hours)
  3. Advanced level (3 hours)
