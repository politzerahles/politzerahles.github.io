For this final activity, read either one of the two readings [PDF] below. (You can do both if you want to, but you are only required to do one.) Then continue on to do a reflection.

  • Basics_of_OT.pdf: Optimality Theory is a completely different way of doing phonology—it's a different approach than what we discussed in the previous activities. It is also a powerful analysis method for explaining, and predicting, patterns that can happen in language learning, language disorders, etc.
  • SLM and PAM.pdf: The Speech Learning Model and Perceptual Assimilation Model are two related models of how second language learners pronounce and perceive the sounds of their second language; both use concepts from phonology to make predictions about what things will cause difficulty for learners.

To finish up, Write a brief reflection about your learning in this module (including your reading about Optimality Theory and/or about the Speech Learning Model and Perceptual Assimilation Model).
